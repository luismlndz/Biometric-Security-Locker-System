STEP 1: DOWNLOAD ANDROID STUDIO AND OPEN THE PROJECT FOLDER "LoginRegister"

STEP 2: DOWNLOAD XAMPP: https://www.apachefriends.org/download.html

STEP 3: CREATE A FOLDER CALLED "LoginRegister" inside 'C:\xampp\htdocs\'

STEP 4: COPY EVERTHING FROM 'php files' FOLDER INTO 'C:\xampp\htdocs\LoginRegister'

STEP 5: OPEN XAMPP AND START 'Apache' AND 'MySQL'

APP SHOULD LET YOU LOG IN NOW




TO CHECK DATABASE INFO, OPEN XAMPP AND CLICK 'Admin' AND THEN CLICK ON 'loginregister' ON THE DROPDOWN
